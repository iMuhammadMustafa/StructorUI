Cards

Cards are structured 
            
              Card
              ├── card-header 
              ├── card-img-top 
              │   ├── card-body
              │   │   ├── card-title
              │   │   ├── card-subtitle
              │   │   ├── card-text
              │   │   ├── card-link
              │   │   └── ...
              ├── card-img-bottom
              └── ...
Cards have no fixed width to start, so they'll naturally fill the full width of its parent element.
